Overview

A mobile application founded and developed by gymrats for gymrats.. for free
Everything for free
Any workout tracker enables you to create templates and track your progress but with a certain limit. GymClub aims to create a platform so that you can create as many templates as you want, record as many workouts as you wish, all for a one-time payment of $0.00
A slight social media twist..
GymClub isn't just a workout tracker, it's a social media platform. Every workout you log is posted on your profile for all your followers to see. Keep up-to-date with your friends, family and fitness models on every workout session they do.
The only workout tracker you'll ever need to track, train & progress.
Online Synchronization
All your templates, records and posts are saved on the cloud. This means that all your data is synched and accessible from any device that has a connection. Whilst you can create and customize your own templates, you are also able to copy and base workouts from other accounts!
Gym-Workout Links
Going to a new gym with a friend but have no clue what machines or exercises you have available to you? With GymClub, you can view all the workouts performed at various gyms across the U.K. giving you a headstart on preparing your session
